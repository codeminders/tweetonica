What is Tweetonica?

Tweetonica is a web application that allows you to manage your Twitter contacts and organize them into groups

What can I do with Tweetonica?

You can:

  • Create new contact groups
  • Rename existing groups
  • Delete groups
  • Move your contacts from one group to another
  • Download OPML file to import it into your favorite RSS reader
  • Have links that point to RSS feeds of group's users posts

How can I move contact to group?

Either drag and drop the contact on desired group at the left or click on the icon next to user's picture to select destination group

What happens if I deleted group that contains contacts?

No contacts are lost. All existing contacts are moved to 'Uncategorized' group

How secure is it?

We take security very seriously. We are using OAuth, so we never ask for your password. You rather enter it on Twitter web site which gives us authorization to access your account. This authorization could be withdrawn anytime from your preferences on Twitter.com web site.
