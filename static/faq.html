What are Phalanges?

Phalanges is a web application that allows you to manage your Twitter contacts and organize them into groups

Top

What Phalanges costs?

Phalanges are FREE

Top

What can I do with Phalanges?

You can:

  • Create new contact groups
  • Rename existing groups
  • Delete groups
  • Move your contacts from one group to another
  • Download OPML file to export it into your favorite RSS reader
  • Have a links that point to RSS feeds of group's users posts

How can I move contact to group?

What happens if I've deleted group that contains contacts?

No contacts are lost. All existing contacts are moved to 'Uncategorized' group
