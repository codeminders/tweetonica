About Tweetonica

Tweetonica allows to manage your Twitter contacts and organize them into groups. Each group gets its own RSS feed which you can add to your favorite RSS reader.

You can:

  • Create groups of contacts
  • Move contacts between groups
  • Rename and delete groups
  • Get RSS feeds, one per group
  • Import all your groups RSS feeds into your RSS reader as handy OPML file
  • You can even subscribe to OPML feed so you reader will be updated with new groups as you create them

A word about security: Tweetonica is using OAuth, so we never ask for your twitter password. You will be redirected to Twitter.com web site and will be authenticated there to give Tweetonica permission to access your account. You can always revoke this permission anytime you want from Twitter web site. All RSS and OPML feeds we generate are password protected.
