About Tweetonica

Tweetonica is a web application developed by Codeminders
that allows to manage your Twitter contacts and organize them into groups.

You can:

  • create new groups of contats
  • move contacts from one group to another
  • rename groups and delete groups

You can download OPML file to import RSS feeds of your contacts posts into your favorite RSS reader.

You can read posts of group's contacts as RSS feed in your favorite RSS reader.
